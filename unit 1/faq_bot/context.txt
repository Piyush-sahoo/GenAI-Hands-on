Company FAQ Policy (Updated)

Overview
This FAQ covers account access, refunds, privacy, and support for the service.

1) Password Reset
- Use the “Forgot Password” link on the login page to receive a reset email.
- Reset links expire after 15 minutes.
- If the email doesn’t arrive within 5 minutes, check Spam/Junk or request a new link.

2) Account Deletion
- Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
- Deletions are processed within 7 business days.
- Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.

3) Refunds
- Monthly plans are refundable within 14 days of purchase.
- Annual plans are refundable within 30 days of purchase.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

4) Data Privacy
- We store only the data needed to deliver the service.
- We never sell personal data to third parties.
- You can request a copy of your data from Settings > Privacy.

5) Support Hours
- Support is available Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm IST.
- Typical response time is within 24 business hours.
